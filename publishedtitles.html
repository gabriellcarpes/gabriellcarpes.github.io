King of Meat

King of Meat is a 4 player co-op dungeon delver published by Amazon Games Studios in 2025

Responsibilities during the project
  • Provided in-depth Level Design feedback and documentation that led to improved pacing throughout the experience
  • Worked in balancing difficulty of levels in order to create a smoother difficulty curve leading into the end game
Feedback from Amazon liaison with NCSoft:

The Team is an integral part for our feedback and review process in 2P. As part of their remit they have provided the King Of Meat team with invaluable game feature deep dives comparisons (for both Player and Create Mode) against competitive titles in the space. Providing Glowmade with this type of side by side analysis has provided a hugely compelling data point for when it comes to prioritization of feature quality improvements

Throne & Liberty

An MMORPG developed by NCSOFT and published in America by Amazon Games Studios

Responsibilities during the project
  • Worked on balancing progression and game economy positively impacting the experience and retention of new players
Feedback from Amazon liaison with NCSoft:

The Team has provided insightful research and analysis that have directly influenced the game's direction and implementation. Their efforts have been particularly impactful in two key areas:

1. Controller-specific changes: The team's in-depth dive into controller-specific optimizations allowed us to significantly enhance the targeting and combat experience for our console players. Their research and recommendations led to clear and actionable steps for our development team to implement, resulting in a more enjoyable gameplay experience.

2. Game comparison showcase: The comprehensive analysis and comparison of combat and movement mechanics between Throne and Liberty and top-tier western MMOs provided us with a valuable benchmark. This showcase highlighted areas for improvement and recently were utilized to gain alignment from NCSoft how improving the combat within Throne and Liberty in the coming months.
